## 1. What Cookies Are
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to support site functionality and preferences.

## 2. Cookie Categories
Runoot uses essential cookies and may use optional analytics cookies based on your consent.
- Essential cookies: login, security, session, language
- Preference cookies: remember settings
- Analytics cookies: improve performance and UX

## 3. Consent Management
You can accept or reject optional cookies in the cookie banner and update preferences later.
Essential cookies remain active because they are required for core functionality.

## 4. Third Parties
Some cookies can be set by trusted third-party providers used for authentication, hosting, or analytics.
For optional analytics, Runoot uses PostHog. Requests are proxied through /ph on the Runoot domain.

## 5. Duration
Cookies can be session cookies or persistent cookies with a defined expiration period.

## 6. Contact
For cookie questions: legal@runoot.com.
